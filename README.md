# Hospital IT War Room Playbook

This repository documents a **practical, real-world IT War Room playbook**
used during hospital commissioning, EMR go-lives, major upgrades,
and critical incidents.

It is designed for **Healthcare IT Heads, CIOs, and Operations Leaders**
responsible for patient safety, system availability, and clinical continuity.

---

## When to activate a War Room
- Hospital commissioning / Day-1 go-live
- EMR / HIS / PACS major rollout or upgrade
- LIS or Radiology cutover
- Network, data center, or storage migration
- Cybersecurity or data availability incidents

---

## Objectives
- Ensure **zero compromise to patient care**
- Reduce Mean Time to Resolution (MTTR)
- Maintain clear communication with clinical leadership
- Provide structured escalation and decision-making
- Capture lessons learned for continuous improvement

---

## What this playbook covers
- War room structure and roles
- Incident classification and escalation
- Downtime and fallback workflows
- Communication templates
- Post-incident review and stabilization
